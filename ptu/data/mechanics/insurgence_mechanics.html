New Moon
  • Dark-Type and Ghost-Type Attacks gain a +5 bonus to Damage Rolls, and Fairy-Type Attacks suffer a -5 Damage penalty.
  • Lunar Cannon, Shadow Force and Phantom Force lose the Set-Up Keyword, and move immediately to resolution effect.
  • Morning Sun, Synthesis, and Moonlight cause the user to regain only 1⁄4 of their full Hit Point value.
  • Users with Shadow Dance will have their initiative doubled.
  • Users with Heliophobia will regain a tick of Hit Points at the end of each turn.
