Updated Stats

For the second time since Gold & Silver introduced the Special Defense stat, multiple Pokémon have had changes to their initial base stats. These changes are relatively minor, just focusing on a slight alteration in one stat. However, these changes do change the way the Pokémon is to be utilised in battle.
