PK Combat

[+Special Attack] [Weapon]

Prerequisites: Telekinetic, Adept Focus

Bind 1 AP – Standard Action

Effect: You may also wield weapons and items with

your Telekinesis capability, as long as you are able to

lift them. This means you may use Items and Melee

Weapons that normally require you to be adjacent to

your target at a range limited only by your Telekinesis.

When wielding Weapons with your Telekinesis, you

qualify for Moves using Focus instead of Combat and

may add your Special Attack instead of your Attack Stat.

The Attacks remain Physical, however.
