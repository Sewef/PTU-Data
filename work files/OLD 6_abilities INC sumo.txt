Ability: Accelerate
Scene x2 - Standard Action, Priority
Effect: The user may activate a damaging Move to which they add STAB with Priority. If the Move hits,
the user adds half their Speed Stat to the Damage Roll. If the Move already had Priority, the attack
gains a +4 Bonus to Accuracy.
---> Replaces Maelstrom Pulse, Leaf Rush, & Gale Wings
